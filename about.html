About

Nike, Inc. is a multinational corporation that is engaged in the design, development, manufacturing, and worldwide marketing and sales of footwear, apparel,

equipment, accessories, and services.The company operates through five main segments: North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, Latin America, and Greater China.

Nike products are manufactured by independent factories located around the world, primarily in Asia. The company works with 700 factories in over 40 countries.

CLOTHES

Nike offers a wide range of clothing items for men, women, and children, including t-shirts, hoodies, jackets, shorts, pants, and more.

SHOES

Nike shoes feature advanced technologies such as Nike Air, Nike React, and Nike Zoom, which provide cushioning for high-impact activities.

ACCESSORIES

Nike accessories are a line of products designed to complement and enhance the performance of their athletic footwear, sports and apparel.
